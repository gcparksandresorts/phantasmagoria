Hallows Eve Inn

Here at the Hallows Eve Inn, snuggle up by the fire and turn on your favorite spooky movie, as you take in the warmth and comfort of Oak Hill’s original hotel. Choose from a variety of dining locations, including, handcrafted cocktails and pizzas at Pumpkin Pizzeria, delights of all flavors at Phantasmagoria Coffee, and concoctions beyond your wildest, witchiest imagination at Juniper’s Mixery & Stone Oven. Visitors can enter Hallows Eve Inn from both inside and outside the Park, making it easy to check in and begin your adventure immediately. Visit for a night, or choose from one of our Rest in Peace Packages to allow entry to and from the Park whenever you like! On your way out, choose from a wide selection of merchandise from all around the Park, here at our very own Phantasmagoria Trading Co.
