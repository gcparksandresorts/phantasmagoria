Dracula's Castle Hotel

Stay at Dracula’s Castle for the ultimate Phantasmagorical experience. At the Resort’s newest hotel, live out your gothic fantasies with fully themed hotel rooms, luxurious interiors, and countless amenities and activities all day and night. Fly down to the vast catacombs for family-friendly dance parties in the day, and watch it transform into a 21+ dance club and bar with live DJs and entertainment featured nightly. The Castle includes access to many eating and dining establishments like the mouthwatering Dracula’s Gargoyle Grill or Bloody Mary’s. Within the walls of Dracula’s Castle, find 6 thrilling attractions, including Katacomb Kart, Dracula’s Castle Drop, and Dracula’s Catacomb Drop. You can also step outside the Castle doors to board The Phantasmagoria Railroad to visit the rest of Hill.
