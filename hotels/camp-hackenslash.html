Camp Hackenslash

If you’re looking for a one-of-a-kind ultimate Halloween experience, stay at Camp Hackenslash in a cozy log cabin, or with your tent and sleeping bag at one of our many outdoor campsites in the Park’s most affordable overnight option. Beware! At night, all of Hackenslash’s most infamous creatures and slashers creep out of the woods on the hunt for unexpecting campers.

Legend tells of a werewolf roaming the woods in the full moonlight. Snuggle around the campfire or settle in for a midnight movie screening of your favorite horror flick at our outdoor movie theater.
