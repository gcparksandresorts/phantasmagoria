Phantasmagoria Lands and Theme Areas

Phantasmagoria is separated into 6 unique lands, each with a special theme and dozens of attractions and activities

Oak Villa

Oak Villa is your gateway to the world of Phantasmagoria. This quaint village square is home to the old Oak on Oak Hill. Here you’ll find Hallow’s Eve Inn, Phantasmagoria Coffee, the original Phantasmagoria Trading Co, or catch a train to begin your grand Halloween adventure.

Dining | Hotel

Witch's Grotto

Nestled in the woods of Trick ‘r’ Treat Hollow is the enchanted Witch’s Grotto where you’ll find the homes of Aradia and the Grotto Coven where they raise a magical variety of plants and animals. Soar over the skies on Aradia’s Enchanted Flight, or take a leisurely boat ride with Aradia’s River Tour. Juniper’s Mixery and Stone Oven boasts a delicious selection of vegan foods, as well as an array of enchanting elixirs for the adults in your coven.

Attractions | Dining | Activities

Trick 'r' Treat Hallow

Step into the world that Halloween calls home in Trick ‘r’ Treat Hollow, the Park’s original land. This classic Halloween town, led by the beloved Mayor Pumpkins, features many can’t-miss rides and attractions, including Mushroom Canyon Mining Co, Halloween Party Trail, Town Hall Carousel, Doctor Frankenstein’s Drop of Doom, and the Phantasmagoria Museum, located just inside the Park’s finest shopping location, the Phantasmagoria Emporium.

Attractions | Dining | Activities

Innsmouth

Are you ready to embark on an unforgettable journey into the heart of the unknown? Explore the otherworldly Innsmouth Island and the strange and beautiful creatures that lurk beneath the depths of Lovecraft Lagoon. With two roller coasters and a variety of family friendly rides and attractions, there’s bound to be something for everyone.

Attractions | Dining | Activities

Harvest Fest

The Trick ‘r’ Treat Hollow Harvest Fest is in full swing. Play carnival games, enjoy classic theme park attractions, or visit the Fortune Teller for a chance encounter with the great beyond. Enjoy a variety of shopping, food, and drinks, including the Harvest Fest Beer Gardens.

Attractions | Dining | Activities

Vampire Village

Fly into the gothic allure of "Vampire Village," a nocturnal haven within Phantasmagoria where the shadows come to life! Embark on the exhilarating flight of your life aboard "The Bat," plunging through batcaves and soaring above Vampire Village's rooftops. Explore the mystic shores of Romania in "Dragons of the Black Sea." Experience the serene yet mesmerizing flight under the blood moon on "Vampiric Ritual," or plunge into the haunted depths of "Dracula’s Castle Drop" and "Dracula’s Catacomb Drop," After your adventures, retreat to the epitome of gothic luxury at "Dracula’s Castle," the Park’s newest hotel, where themed rooms, gourmet dining at Dracula’s Gargoyle Grill, and pulsating nightlife at the 21+ dance club await, ensuring an unforgettable, all-encompassing Phantasmagorical experience under the moonlit skies.

Camp Hackenslash

At Camp Hackenslash, every moment is a chance for discovery and exhilaration. Soar above campgrounds on the "Hackenslash Ski Lift," taking in breathtaking vistas of the surrounding wilderness. Feel the adrenaline rush on the thrilling spins of the "Hackenslash Twister," nestled tightly within the campsite, and brave the secrets of the "Buzzsaw Falls" where brave souls may uncover the truth behind the vanishing workers at the logging company. Embrace the rustic charm of Camp Hackenslash, offering cozy log cabins and outdoor campsites for an immersive experience. As night falls, prepare for encounters with legendary creatures and enigmatic wanderers said to roam the woods under the moon's glow. Gather around the crackling campfire, savor midnight movie screenings at the outdoor theater, and relish the camaraderie of fellow adventurers.
