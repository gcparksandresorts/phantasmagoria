Phantasmagoria Lands and Theme Areas

Phantasmagoria is separated into 6 unique lands, each with a special theme and dozens of attractions and activities

Innsmouth

Entrance Plaza of Park, featuring the original giant Oak Tree. This square features the Park entrance, a train station, Hollow’s Eve Inn, food & drink, and the entrances to Trick ‘r’ Treat Hollow, Innsmouth, and Vampire Village.
